Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 (8 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Place lasagna noodles into a deep bowl and cover with very hot tap water; let soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Beat egg in a bowl and stir ricotta cheese, 2 tablespoons parsley, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and nutmeg into egg until thoroughly combined.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Cover bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish with 1 cup sauce.
  5. Layer 4 soaked lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture, 1/3 of the shredded mozzarella cheese, and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese in the dish.
  6. Repeat layers twice more, ending with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  7. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
  8. Bake until lasagna noodles are tender and casserole is bubbling, about 50 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake until cheese topping is lightly browned, 15 to 20 more minutes. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
